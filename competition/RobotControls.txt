// Claw Controls

B             claw (open/close)
Y             arm (up/down)
dpad_up       launch drone and reload
right_trigger lift extend
left_trigger  lift contract

// Drive Controls

right_stick_y drive
right_stick_x strafe
left_stick_x  turn
right_bumper  move slower

dpad_right    turn 90 (15 with right_bumper)
dpad_left     turn 90 (15 with right_bumper)
